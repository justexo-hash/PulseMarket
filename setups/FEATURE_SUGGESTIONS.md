# Feature Suggestions for Polymeme

## High-Impact Features (Recommended to Add)

### 1. **Market Sorting & Advanced Filters** ⭐
**Impact**: High | **Effort**: Medium
- Sort by: Volume, Recent Activity, Probability, Created Date
- Filter by: Status (active/resolved), Date Range, Pool Size
- Quick filters: "High Volume", "Trending", "Ending Soon"

**Why**: Helps users discover interesting markets faster

---

### 2. **Market Activity Feed / Dashboard** ⭐
**Impact**: High | **Effort**: Medium
- Show recent bets across all markets
- Recent market resolutions
- High-value bet alerts ("Someone just bet 10 SOL!")
- Market creation notifications

**Why**: Creates engagement, shows platform activity

---

### 3. **Market Expiration Dates** ⭐
**Impact**: High | **Effort**: Low-Medium
- Auto-close markets after a deadline
- Show countdown timers
- "Ending Soon" badge on cards
- Email/notification reminders

**Why**: Creates urgency, prevents stale markets

---

### 4. **Probability History Charts** ⭐
**Impact**: Medium-High | **Effort**: Medium
- Graph showing how probability changed over time
- Shows on market detail page
- Helps users see betting trends
- Useful for making informed bets

**Why**: Visual data helps users make better decisions

---

### 5. **User Leaderboards** ⭐
**Impact**: High | **Effort**: Medium
- Top traders by profit
- Most accurate predictors
- Biggest winners
- Weekly/monthly leaderboards
- Badges/achievements

**Why**: Gamification increases engagement

---

### 6. **Market Descriptions & Metadata** 
**Impact**: Medium | **Effort**: Low
- Add description field to markets
- Market tags/labels
- Creator attribution
- Market rules/clarifications
- Reference links

**Why**: Makes markets more informative

---

### 7. **Watchlist / Following Markets**
**Impact**: Medium | **Effort**: Medium
- Let users "watch" markets they're interested in
- Show watched markets in a dedicated section
- Notifications when watched markets resolve
- Quick access to markets you care about

**Why**: Increases user retention

---

### 8. **Bet History Details Page**
**Impact**: Low-Medium | **Effort**: Low
- Show all your bets on a market
- View bet timing and probability at that moment
- See potential vs. actual returns
- Export bet history

**Why**: Better tracking and analysis

---

### 9. **Share Market Links**
**Impact**: Low-Medium | **Effort**: Low
- Share button on market cards
- Generate shareable links
- Social media preview cards (Open Graph)
- Referral tracking

**Why**: Viral growth potential

---

### 10. **Market Analytics Dashboard**
**Impact**: Medium | **Effort**: Medium-High
- Platform-wide statistics
- Total volume across all markets
- Most active markets
- Category breakdown
- Trading volume charts

**Why**: Shows platform health and activity

---

## Nice-to-Have Features (Lower Priority)

### 11. **User Profiles / Public Pages**
- View other users' public betting history
- User stats (win rate, total profit)
- Follow other traders

### 12. **Comments / Discussion on Markets**
- Users can discuss markets
- Share analysis and reasoning
- Community engagement

### 13. **Market Templates**
- Pre-made market types
- Quick market creation
- Common event templates (sports, elections, etc.)

### 14. **Mobile App / PWA**
- Progressive Web App for mobile
- Push notifications
- Offline viewing

### 15. **API / Export**
- Public API for market data
- CSV export of transactions
- Integration with other tools

---

## Recommended Implementation Order

1. **Market Expiration Dates** (Easiest, high value)
2. **Market Sorting** (Quick win, improves UX)
3. **Activity Feed** (Engagement driver)
4. **Probability History Charts** (Visual appeal)
5. **Leaderboards** (Gamification)
